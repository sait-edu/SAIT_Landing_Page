About SAIT

The School of AI and Technology (SAIT) is dedicated to providing high-quality education in the fields of programming, artificial intelligence, and technology. Located in Alexandria, Egypt, our institution has been at the forefront of technological education since its inception.

Our mission is to empower students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving tech industry. With a team of experienced instructors led by Abdullah Mahmoud, we offer a supportive learning environment that fosters creativity and innovation.
